The Product Leadership Gap

The Challenge You're Facing

You're a successful Product Manager, but stepping into leadership feels like a completely different game. Suddenly you're not just shipping features—you're setting vision, managing up to executives, building team culture, and navigating complex organizational dynamics. Now add in the need to keep pace with AI technologies while identifying the right use case for leveraging AI, all while learning, applying and explaining the benefits to executives and stakeholders.

As you step into leadership roles, you will lead other product managers (and maybe other roles such as software engineers) of varying levels of seniority. Getting the team to buy into the vision and mission is a key challenge.

Most PMs are promoted to leadership roles without proper training, leaving them unprepared for the challenges of leading at scale.

The Solution: Systematic Leadership Development

This isn't another generic leadership course. This is the systematic approach to product leadership learned and refined over years of building and scaling at Amazon and now Walmart. We will also dedicate time to learn about how you can leverage AI to accelerate your success as a Product Leader. Every single topic is grounded in real world experience of your coach. No BS, no fluff.

By the end of 6 weeks, you'll have the confidence and capabilities to lead product organizations of any size, drive innovation at scale, and create lasting impact.

6-Week Product Leadership Curriculum

1

Product Strategy & Vision

Master the art of creating compelling product visions that inspire and align stakeholders. Learn strategic frameworks for market analysis, competitive positioning, and opportunity assessment. Practice building and communicating multi-year product strategies that drive business impact.

2

Technical Fluency & Data Driven Decision Making

Develop the technical depth needed to lead engineering teams effectively. Learn to evaluate technical trade-offs, understand system architecture decisions, and use data to drive product decisions. Master frameworks for working with technical teams and making informed technology choices.

3

AI Fluency & Experience Based Use Cases Addressed by AI

Build practical AI fluency through real-world case studies from Amazon and Walmart. Learn to identify legitimate AI opportunities, evaluate AI vs traditional solutions, and understand when AI adds genuine value. Walk through actual AI implementations and their business impact.

4

Systems Thinking for Product Leaders

Develop the ability to see the bigger picture and understand complex interdependencies. Learn to design products that scale, anticipate unintended consequences, and create sustainable competitive advantages through systems-level thinking and architecture.

5

The Art of Communication & Cross Functional Collaboration

Master the communication skills that separate great product leaders from good ones. Learn to influence without authority, present to executives, facilitate difficult conversations, and build alignment across diverse teams and stakeholders.

6

Leadership & Team Management

Learn to build and lead high-performing product teams at scale. Cover hiring frameworks, performance management, career development, and creating inclusive team cultures. Develop your personal leadership style and 90-day action plan.
